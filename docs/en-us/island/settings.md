## Sleep System
**Implemented via the [NoPhantomSleep](https://GitHub.com/Rain-Serenity/NoPhantomSleep) plugin. As long as you right-click a bed at night, it will count as having passed the entire night, and no phantoms will spawn around you.** <br>

**Our view distance is set to 10 chunks.**

## Obtaining Sunflowers
**In sunflower plains biomes, using bone meal on grass blocks has a 10% chance to replace 30% of flowers and grass within a 9×9 area with sunflowers.**
## Obtaining Coral
**When the biome is warm ocean, using bone meal on underwater blocks can randomly generate coral (blocks, fans)** <br>
**Probabilities:**
| Item         | Probability |
|--------------|-------------|
| Sea Grass    | 60%         |
| Coral Block  | 15%         |
| Coral Plant  | 15%         |
| Coral Fan    | 10%         |
## Natural Guardian Spawning
**In deep cold ocean biomes, natural squid/cod/salmon/pufferfish/tropical fish spawns are automatically replaced with guardians.**
## JEI Support
**This server uses the custom plugin [JEIBukkit](https://GitHub.com/Rain-Serenity/JEIBukkit), which automatically syncs recipes to your client when you join.**

## Sit Anywhere
**Sneak at the edge of a block until you hear feedback, then release to sit down.**
## Iron Block Elevator & Rail Teleport
**Iron Block Elevator: Place iron blocks at different Y levels with the same XZ coordinates, with enough space above. Sneak on the iron block to teleport down, jump to teleport up.**
**Rail Teleport: Sneak on rails to teleport to the other end of the rail.**
